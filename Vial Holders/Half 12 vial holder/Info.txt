### Purpose
This holder can be used to hold twelve (12) 20 mL scintillation vials in a single
slot of the OT2 pipetting robot

### Use specifics
In order to make a single holder, two of these .stl files will need to be printed,
they then wedge together in a single slot holder and can hold 12 vials

### Measurements per 1 print
* Height:19.5 mm
* Length:85.87 mm
* Width: 63.85 mm
* Wells: 6
* Well Depth: 17.21 mm
* Well Diameter: 27.85 mm

### Well layout
[[A1 A2 A3 A4],
[B1 B2 B3 B4],
[C1 C2 C3 C4],

### Well Pipetting Order:
[A1, B1, C1, A2, B2, C2, A3, B3, C3, A4, B4, C4]
